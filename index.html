Aura Control Oy

We design and implemet automation and PLC systems

Aura Control Oy was founded in spring 1998. The company designs and implements automation and PLC systems. Aura Control is pioneer in remote operation of automation systems. We can get you software updates and system support through internet or telephone lines. State of the art knowledge at Aura Control will make sure that you get the best out of your process. We will make sure that your controllers are tuned properly and we can also design modern predictive or fuzzy controllers if required. Aura Control Oy is system partner of Beijer Electronics which will give us the best possible technical support, quick delivery times and competitive prices. We offer full scale of Mitsubishi MELSEC PLC:s, FactoryLink ECS control room software, OD process automation system and Beijer Electronics E series operating panels. Aura Control Oy offers electrical, PLC and automation system commissioning support. We have commissioned quite a number of plants around the world.
